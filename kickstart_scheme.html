The KickStart Scheme

What Is The Scheme All About?

Is funding young employees a problem for your business? Not a problem: the Scheme has you covered and Vasave is acting as an official government gateway.

We are taking part in this scheme in order to give young people the chance to boost their employability and gain the crucial experience needed in the workforce.

Benefits For Small/Medium Businesses

With Vasave Business Solutions, small businesses will get the opportunity to access motivated young individuals keen on joining companies to gain relevant experience and knowledge, benefiting both the workforce and the employee.

With the Kickstart Scheme, you are entitled to government funding if the job placement covers the following:

1.   The relevant National Minimum Wage for 25 hours a week.
2.  The associated employer National Insurance contributions.

3. Employer minimum automatic enrolment contributions.

4. Funding also available to support young people to develop new skills and help them into sustained employment after they have completed their kickstart funded job
